Test Overview

The digital SAT,PSAT,/NMSQT, PSAT 10, and PSAT 8/9 each consist of a Reading and Writing section and a Math section.

Section 1: Reading and Writing (54 Questions)

there are two modules in the Reading and Writing section. Each module has 27 questions

Each question includes one or more passages, which may include a table or graph. Read each passage and answer carefully, and then choose the best answer to the question based on the the passage(s). All questions in this section are multiple choice with four answer choices. Each questions has a single best answer.

Section 2: Math (44 Questions)

there are two modules in the Math section. Each module has 22 questions.

Math

Within each section, the two modules are timed separetely. If time permits, you can review your answers in a given module. When the time reaches zero, you will automatically move on. Once you move on from any module, you cannot return to it

Section Directions

Directions for the Reading and Writing and Math sections can be found in the Help section under More at the top right of your testing screen
